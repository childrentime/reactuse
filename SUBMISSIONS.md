## How to update this file

After every submission attempt (auto or manual), append a row to **✅ Submitted** with the date, platform, receipt URL/ID, and any notes. When a submission is approved, accepted, or rejected, move the row to the appropriate section. The `publish-hashnode` skill writes here automatically; OpenCLI flows write here at the end of each successful submission.
